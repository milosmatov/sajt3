About us

Born in 1979. in Čačak. He went to a kindergarten called "Nadežda Petrović" for three years. Father Miodrag and mother Milinka are the people who brought him into this world with joy. Living relatives include his aunt and his aunt's brother, who were born in Čačak and live further away from him, where he is now settled. His maternal grandmother took care of him and his sister. Ljubić the most, the village is close, it almost belongs to the city center, but it was on a hill where there is a nice view of the city panorama. Grandma passed away in 2000. and on a church holiday celebrated by doctors in Serbia. Every New Year, his sister and he received packages from Santa and there were lots of sweets and some toys. As a child before elementary school, he was very mischievous and even liked to fight, of course, only with his hands. He always climbed to the top of the cherry tree and picked the most beautiful ones, which he later shared with his friends who were not so brave as to climb so high on the tree. Summer was reserved for the sea, where they went to a resort that belonged to Elektrodistribucija Čačak in Bečići most often, and during the eighties of the twentieth century they spent fifteen days there. The winter was spent on Kopaonik, a mountain in Serbia, where he and his sister learned to ski on the Mali Karaman track, but also on Pančićev vrh.
